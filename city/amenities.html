Directions

Krakowskie Przedmiescie 64, Warsaw, Poland

From the South-West Poland International Airport follow the Terminal Access Road from the airport. After 1.6 miles, turn right to Krakowskie in the south. After five minutes of walking you should see Carrefour market, you can tell by a large sign. Your current position will be "Plac Danielewicza". There will be a bus stop near the entrance to the shopping mall. At this point, you can reach your destination, which is "Rotunda Cegielskiego" by choosing bus no. 128, 403, but keep in mind that you'll have to make a transfer on 'Sarmacka' bus stop. From there go by bus no. 503, which will take you directly to your destination point.
